About Us


    Welcome to TipCalculator.com, your trusted companion for calculating the perfect tip at your favorite restaurants and bars. Our mission is to make dining out a more enjoyable and stress-free experience by providing a simple and intuitive tool for calculating the right amount to leave for your server.


    Our Story
    TipCalculator.com was founded by a group of friends who were tired of the hassle and uncertainty that came with trying to figure out the right tip. They wanted a solution that was easy to use, accurate, and accessible to everyone. After months of development, our team created a user-friendly tip calculator that can be used anywhere, at any time.
